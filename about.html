About Us

Betty foods is a customer focused food services company established in 2008, we have since attained a front line position in the highly competitive restaurants service industry. We engaged professional food experts from diverse backgrounds and cultures to achieve the true taste our customers deserve. Our foods are produced under the most hygienic conditions and we make use of only most modern and proven equipment for our extensive, diverse and imaginable array of foods. Our tastefully furnished and relaxed restaurants environment provide the perfect ambience to enjoy your eating experience. Also our sumptuous foods are served by well-mannered and decent staffs.

We open from 7am to 11pm; 7days a week to enable us give maximum services to our numerous and esteemed customers. Our car parks are designed to accommodate large number of cars. We also provide adequate security and good traffic control for our customers. Apart from food we provide fantasy land, an ultra-modern and fully air-conditioned arcade and games centre for children, old and young with modern and latest arcade and games equipment in an expanse space at our Lekki and Ikeja mall. It is an ideal choice for family outing where space is allocated for children birthday celebrations. We also provide Outdoor Catering Services for Parties, Annual General Meetings, Corporate Staff Canteen and Private Homes.

Our Vision:

Betty foods was conceived by country builders, to develop a first class Fast Food, Chinese and Continental restaurants of unbeatable taste and quality that will accommodate low, middle and high class customers, in the food and hospitality industry where all nationals can always feel at home.

Our Mission:

To experience the excitement of offering unparalleled quality in service delivery and products development to customers under the best hygienic condition.
